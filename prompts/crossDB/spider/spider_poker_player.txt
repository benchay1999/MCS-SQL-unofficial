# [poker_player]
- Poker_Player_ID (number): Unique identifier for each poker player
- People_ID (number): Identifier linking the poker player to their personal details
- Final_Table_Made (number): Number of times the player has made it to the final table
- Best_Finish (number): Highest position finished in a tournament
- Money_Rank (number): Ranking of the player based on earnings
- Earnings (number): Total career earnings of the player in USD

# [people]
- People_ID (number): Unique identifier for each person
- Nationality (text): Nationality of the person
- Name (text): Full name of the person
- Birth_Date (text): Date of birth of the person
- Height (number): Height of the person in centimeters
