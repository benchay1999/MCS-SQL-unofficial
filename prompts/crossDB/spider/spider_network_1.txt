# [Highschooler]
- ID (number): Unique identifier for each high school student
- name (text): The student's full name
- grade (number): The grade level of the student

# [Friend]
- student_id (number): Identifier for the student who is friends with another student
- friend_id (number): Identifier for the student who is the friend of the first student

# [Likes]
- student_id (number): Identifier of the student who likes another student
- liked_id (number): Identifier of the student who is liked by the first student
