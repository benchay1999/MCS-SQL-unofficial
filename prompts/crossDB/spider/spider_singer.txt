# [singer]
- Singer_ID (number): Unique identifier for each singer
- Name (text): Full name of the singer
- Birth_Year (number): Year the singer was born
- Net_Worth_Millions (number): Estimated net worth of the singer in millions of dollars
- Citizenship (text): Country of citizenship for the singer

# [song]
- Song_ID (number): Unique identifier for each song
- Title (text): Name of the song
- Singer_ID (number): Identifier linking the song to its singer
- Sales (number): Number of copies sold
- Highest_Position (number): Highest chart position reached by the song
